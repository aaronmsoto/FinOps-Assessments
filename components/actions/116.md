# 116: Prioritize Actions & Publish Roadmap

**Slug:** 116-actions-roadmap

**Implementation Types:**

* None

## Descriptive Statement

Prioritize actions; publish an improvement roadmap with owners/dates.

## Supplemental Guidance

Weights are about maturity priority and which actions are important to the business' goals.
Here, priority is about what to work on first to get the most ROI from work to close the maturity gaps.
Ensure that planned work reflects the realities of team development lifecycles, such as agile, waterfall, etc.

## Scoring Guide

**Weight:** 1.0

### Formula

1. Invite subject matter experts (SMEs) and leadership to determine which gaps to address first
2. Determine owners for addressing each of the prioritized gaps
3. Owners will work with relevant teams to scope out how to meet the target score for each gap
4. Work is then scheduled in a roadmap with deadlines that can be tracked at the leadership level

### Scoring Details

**Score Type:** sequential

| Score | Condition |
| ----- | --------- |
| 0 | No items completed |
| 3 | item 1 completed |
| 5 | items 1, and 2 completed |
| 8 | items 1, 2, and 3 completed |
| 10 | items 1, 2, 3, and 4 completed |
